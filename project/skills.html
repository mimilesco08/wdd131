💡 Pro Training Tips

Consistency is Key

Practice a little every day rather than long sessions once a week. Muscle memory develops through repetition.

Quality over Quantity

Focus on perfect form rather than speed. Build the right habits from the beginning.

Practice Game Situations

Always practice drills at game speed and in game-like situations for maximum transfer to real play.
